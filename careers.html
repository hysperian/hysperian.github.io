Join Our Team at Ian's Chophouse

Ian's Chophouse is hiring passionate, skilled, and career-driven individuals to join our dedicated team. We offer a unique opportunity to work with top industry professionals, explore culinary techniques, and grow your career in high-end dining.

At Ian's Chophouse, we’re pushing the boundaries of what a chophouse can be. If you have a love for quality ingredients, a passion for learning, and a drive to showcase your culinary talent, we’d love to meet you.

What We Offer:

  • Hands-on experience with premium beef aging and fabrication, stock and sauce making, smoking, and more.
  • Unique seafood programs featuring local oysters, crabs, shrimp, and more.
  • Opportunities to shine at our oyster and raw bar.
  • Full-time, part-time, hourly, and salaried positions are available.
